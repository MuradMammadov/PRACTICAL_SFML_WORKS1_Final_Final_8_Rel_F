User manual of mine-race

You are playing against an ai.

The rules of the game are as follows:
-There is 100 meter tunnel that needs to be mine.
-You and ai take turns mining using 1 of three offered tools
-pickaxe will mine 5 meters ahead
-drill will mine 10 meters ahead
-thermonuclear bomb will mine 30 meters ahead
-the one who reaches the end of the tunnel wins(If your last turn mines more than remaining distance it still counts as win)

Initially you choose whether you would like to go 1st or 2nd.(Though if you go 2nd you likely won't win)
